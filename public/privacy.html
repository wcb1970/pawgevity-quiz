Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026
Overview
Our commitment to your privacy

Pawgevity ("we," "us," or "our") operates the wellness profile quiz at check.pawgevitywellness.com and the website at pawgevitywellness.com. We take your privacy seriously.

This policy explains what information we collect when you use our services, why we collect it, and how we use it. We don't sell your personal information. We don't share it with advertisers. We use it only to deliver the wellness overview you asked for and to send you follow-up guidance you can unsubscribe from at any time.

If you have questions about this policy or want to request deletion of your data, email us at [email protected] and we'll respond within 5 business days.

Section 1
What information we collect

When you complete the Pawgevity Wellness Profile quiz, we collect the following information:

  • Your email address — provided voluntarily at the email gate screen
  • Your dog's name — entered in the first quiz question
  • Your dog's breed — selected from the quiz options
  • Your dog's age range — selected from the quiz options
  • Your primary area of concern — selected from the quiz options
  • Your dog's current supplement routine — selected from the quiz options
  • Observable signals you've noticed — selected from the quiz options
  • Approximate time since last vet visit — selected from the quiz options

We also collect standard technical information that web servers collect automatically, including your approximate location (city/country level), browser type, device type, and the pages you visit. This information is collected in aggregate and is not linked to your personal identity.

Section 2
Why we collect it and how we use it

We collect your email address and quiz responses for the following purposes:

  • To deliver your wellness overview — your quiz responses are used to generate and display your personalized 5-pillar wellness profile on screen
  • To send your results by email — your complete overview, practical suggestions, and follow-up guidance are sent to the email address you provide
  • To send follow-up email guidance — we may send a short series of educational emails related to your dog's wellness areas. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email
  • To personalize our communications — we use your dog's name, breed, age, and primary concern area to personalize the content we send you
  • To improve our service — aggregated, anonymized quiz response data may be used to understand how owners use the quiz and to improve our wellness guidance

We do not use your information for automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects. Our wellness profile is a general informational tool, not a diagnostic system.

Section 3
Who we share your information with

We share your information only with the service providers necessary to operate our quiz and deliver your results:

  • Klaviyo — our email marketing platform. Your email address and dog profile data (name, breed, age, concern area, routine, signals) are stored in Klaviyo and used to send your wellness overview and follow-up emails. Klaviyo's privacy policy is available at klaviyo.com/legal/privacy.
  • Vercel — our website hosting provider. Vercel processes requests to our website and may collect standard server log data. Vercel's privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Anthropic — if you use any AI-powered features within the app, your inputs may be processed by Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not share your information with advertisers.

Section 4
Email communications and unsubscribing

By providing your email address and completing the quiz, you consent to receive your wellness overview and a short follow-up email series from Pawgevity. These emails contain wellness guidance related to the areas identified in your profile.

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. Clicking it will immediately remove you from our mailing list. You can also email [email protected] to request removal.

We will not send you promotional emails unrelated to dog wellness without your explicit consent.

Section 5
Your rights and data choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — you can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — you can request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — you can request that we delete your personal information. We will remove your data from our systems and from Klaviyo within 30 days
  • Unsubscribe — you can opt out of email communications at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send
  • Portability — you can request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at [email protected]. We will respond within 5 business days and fulfill your request within 30 days.

If you are located in California, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information).

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our lawful basis for processing your personal data is your consent (provided when you submit your email address) and our legitimate interest in delivering the service you requested.

Section 6
Cookies and tracking

Our quiz website uses minimal tracking. We may use standard web analytics tools to understand aggregate visitor behavior (pages visited, time on site, device type). This data is not linked to your personal identity.

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels that follow you across other websites. We do not use retargeting advertising that uses your quiz responses or email address to show you ads elsewhere.

Section 7
Data security and retention

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your personal information. Your data is stored on Klaviyo's secure servers, which use industry-standard encryption. Our website is served over HTTPS.

We retain your email address and dog profile data for as long as you remain subscribed to our email list, or until you request deletion. If you unsubscribe, your email is removed from our active mailing lists. If you request full deletion, we will remove your data from all systems within 30 days.

No method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Section 8
Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at [email protected] and we will delete it promptly.

Section 9
A note about our wellness content

The Pawgevity Wellness Profile is a general wellness awareness tool. It is not a veterinary diagnostic service, and the information we collect is used only to generate a general informational overview — not a clinical assessment of your dog.

We do not share your wellness profile data with veterinarians, insurance companies, or any third party without your explicit consent.

This profile is for general informational purposes only and does not constitute veterinary medical advice. Always consult a licensed veterinarian for guidance on your dog's health.

Section 10
Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes, we will notify active subscribers by email.

Continued use of our services after any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
